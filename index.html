The Teacher's Planner App

Welcome to the Teacher's Planner App - developed by a former teacher! In a teacher's life, there is such a large amount of things to do! This app will allow teachers to add tasks and add tabs for those tasks to fall under. Once those tasks are completed, those tasks can then be deleted by checking the boxes. Tasks and tabs can also be rearranged by order of priority simply by dragging. This app will help keep you organized and prepared!
